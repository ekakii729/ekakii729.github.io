About Us

At ABC Solutions, we believe that every space has a story waiting to be told. Founded with a passion for transforming ordinary rooms into extraordinary experiences, our mission is to bring creativity, comfort, and character into every design. Whether it’s a cozy home, a chic office, or a vibrant commercial space, we blend art and functionality to craft environments that inspire and delight. Our purpose goes beyond aesthetics; we’re here to create spaces that reflect your personality, meet your needs, and elevate your daily life. Rooted in values of creativity, sustainability, and customer-centricity, we prioritize innovative ideas, eco-friendly practices, and a deep commitment to making your vision come to life. At ABC Solutions, we’re not just designing interiors—we’re crafting dreams, one room at a time. Let us help you create a space that feels uniquely, beautifully you!

Our Team
  • CEO Sophia Bennett

    • Sophia founded ABC Solutions after a decade in the design industry, where she felt limited by cookie-cutter solutions and uninspired spaces. Her dream was to create a company that combined cutting-edge creativity with a personal touch. Sophia’s passion lies in turning her clients’ wildest design dreams into reality, from bold concepts to intimate details. As CEO, she oversees the company’s direction, leads the design team, and ensures every project aligns with ABC Solutions’ core values. She’s the heart and soul of the company, driven by her love for transforming spaces and connecting with clients.

  • Lead Designer Liam Carter

    • Liam joined ABC Solutions three years ago after being inspired by Sophia’s vision of merging art with sustainability. With a background in architectural design and a passion for eco-friendly practices, Liam specializes in sourcing sustainable materials and creating spaces that are both stunning and environmentally responsible. He works closely with clients to bring their ideas to life while ensuring each project minimizes its ecological footprint. For Liam, every design is a chance to make the world a little greener, one room at a time.

  • Client Relations Manager Maya Patel

    • Maya became part of ABC Solutions after experiencing the company’s magic firsthand as a client. With her background in hospitality and her love for design, she was drawn to the company’s unique blend of artistry and customer focus. Now, Maya is the friendly face behind every project, guiding clients from their first consultation to the final reveal. Her passion lies in building strong relationships and making the design process smooth, fun, and stress-free for everyone involved. She’s all about making sure clients feel heard, valued, and thrilled with the results.
